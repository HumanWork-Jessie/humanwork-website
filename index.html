A Preview

Programs in
Development

Each program challenges a conventional management assumption and replaces it with something grounded in how people actually work.

Distributed and Hybrid Leadership

Leading at a Distance

The old model of management was built for physical proximity. Distance did not break leadership — it exposed what was already broken. This program rebuilds it for wherever your people actually are.

Communication

COMMUNICATE: Inspiring People to Actually Listen

Most communication training teaches you how to talk. This one teaches you how to move people. Built on an original Human Work framework grounded in how influence actually works.

Time, Attention and Energy

Choosing What Matters

Stop managing time — you cannot. Start leading with clarity about what and who actually deserves your energy. Built on the INTENT framework for conscious decision-making.

Conflict and Repair

Navigating Conflict

Most leaders manage conflict. This program teaches you to resolve it — and repair what broke. Built on the SURFACE, RESOLVE, REPAIR architecture for teams doing real work.

20% Social Learning
