What to Expect

Speaking Topics
in Development

The Human Work: Leadership as Identity Practice

The leader you are and the life you have lived are not two different things. This keynote challenges the myth of the professional self and explores what happens when leaders stop performing and start practicing.

Systems We Inherited: Unlearning What the Workplace Taught Us

The 40-hour week, the performance review, the org chart — these carry assumptions about who belongs, who leads, and what success means. This talk names what we have absorbed without choosing it, and charts a path forward.

Belonging Isn't Soft: The ROI of Human-Centered Leadership

Belonging is not a feelings conversation. It is a performance conversation. This talk connects the neuroscience of psychological safety to measurable outcomes — and gives leaders concrete language for building it.

Leading at a Distance: The New Rules of Proximity and Trust

Distance did not break leadership — it exposed what was already broken. This talk reframes remote and hybrid leadership as an opportunity to rebuild something better, not just replicate the old model on a video call.
